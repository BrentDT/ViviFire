Objects

This article gives links to other articles that give information about the ViviFire Run-time objects. These objects supply many methods, properties, and events that you can use in your programs.

ViviFire run-time objects

#Debug
Lets you more easily debug your programs.
Error
Contains information about run-time errors.
#Log
Does operations on the log file.
